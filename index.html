Frequently Asked Questions

What is Farm2U?

Farm2U is a project that aims to both support local farmers and their families as well as provide consumers with fresh organic products.

Who is it for?

Farm2U was created to both help farmers and consumers. It allows small farmers to sell their products and allows the consumers to enjoy the produce from the farmers Farm2U is simply the intermediary.

How do I use it?

Farmers can register using the Farm2U mobile app and consumers can order using this website.
